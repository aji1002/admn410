Policies


Financing

We realize that some products that your pet needs, may not fit into your budget, that's why we created a financing program. Just ask any employee about the financing option, and they will set you up with a flat rate of 3.99% APR. The sooner you pay it off, the less interest you are charged.

Delivery

Some of our products may be to large or heavy to lift out of the store, that's why we allow for same day delivery. Any item over 75 lbs. is eligible for same day delivery, anywhere within a 15 mile radius of the store. The rate is only $15.

Return Policies

The return policy at Durham House of Pets, allows for the return of any unopened item, up to 30 days from the purchase date, with a receipt. Returns outside of the 30 day period, calls for an in-store credit.
